Tutorial

How To Measure Your Fit

Have a friend help you take these measurements using a standard flexible measuring tape. Keep the tape level and snug, but not tight.

01. Bust Circumference

Wrap the tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, horizontal to the floor. Relax your arms down and breathe normally.

02. Underbust Waist

Wrap the tape measure directly beneath your bust where the lower band of your blouse would sit. Make sure it sits firm.

03. Front/Back Deepness

Measure vertically from the shoulder seam straight down to where you want the neck cutout to end (e.g. 6.5 inches for standard front U-neck).

04. Sleeve Length & Armhole

Measure shoulder tip down your arm for length (e.g. 10" for elbow). Wrap around biceps to measure armhole sleeve cuffs.
