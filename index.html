Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer doorstep blouse measurements? ▾

Yes. Our professional styling masters visit addresses across Visakhapatnam and Pendurthi to take measurements and collect your saree fabrics.

What is the standard tailoring delivery speed? ▾

Our standard tailoring is completed in 5-7 days. We also offer an Express 48-Hour tailoring service with home pickup.

What happens if the stitched blouse does not fit perfectly? ▾

We provide one year of free adjustments. Our executioners will collect the blouse, perform adjustments at our Visakhapatnam workshop, and deliver it back to you.
