Local Tailoring FAQs

Do you collect blouse fabric directly from my house?

Yes. If you book a "Blouse Pickup" or "Home Measurement" service, our delivery executive will collect your saree fabrics and sample blouses directly from your doorstep in Pendurthi and nearby areas.

Do you charge extra for heavy bridal designs?

Stitching charges are fixed (starting at ₹1,800). Premium embroidery additions like Maggam work or zardozi are calculated dynamically inside our online Customization Studio.
