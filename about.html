The Brand

Stitching heritage with modern convenience.

Saviaa was born in Pendurthi, Visakhapatnam out of a simple realization: every woman deserves a perfectly fitting blouse, tailored exactly to her design taste, without having to navigate local boutique delays, pricing spikes, or generic fittings.

We believe customization is not a luxury privilege. By merging digital interactive tailoring templates with our skilled network of traditional tailoring artisans in Visakhapatnam and Hyderabad, we offer flawless fits and custom choices at affordable price points.

No loose linings, no restrictive shoulders. Just hand-tailored garments built to your specifications and delivered straight to your home.

Premium Affordable

We eliminate the middle-man showroom markups. We purchase raw silks and cotton handlooms directly from weavers in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, keeping custom tailoring affordable.

Artisan Driven

Every Maggam handwork pattern and Zardozi embroidery motif is sewn by local women tailors in Visakhapatnam, providing sustainable livelihood support directly to families.

The Workshop

Crafted in Pendurthi, Vizag

Our centralized stitching hub in Pendurthi, Visakhapatnam houses modern measuring tables, specialized stitching machinery, and custom design layouts. This workspace is run by master cutters and tailors with over 20 years of experience in bridal fittings, ensuring absolute accuracy down to the millimeter.
