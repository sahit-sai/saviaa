Our Story & Craft: Handcrafted Saree Blouses in Visakhapatnam

SAVIAA was founded with a simple objective: to bring dignity, precision, and ease to saree blouse tailoring. We believe that a blouse is not merely a companion piece to a saree, but an engineered garment that defines grace and comfort.

Local Heritage

Artisanal Tailoring Rooted in Local Telugu Tradition

Operating out of Pendurthi Bypass Junction in Visakhapatnam, our workshop employs master tailors who have dedicated decades to Indian ethnic garment construction. Every fabric piece is measured, cut, and stitched with double-reinforced lining to prevent seam slippage.

From traditional Kanchipuram silk blouses to contemporary boat-neck and elbow-sleeve Maggam embroidery styles, our signature collections reflect modern South Indian fashion sensibilities.

Doorstep Convenience

Precision Sizing & Doorstep Measurement Expertise

Recognizing the busy schedules of women in Visakhapatnam, SAVIAA introduced doorstep measurement visits. Our female tailoring representatives bring sample necklines, tape measures, and fabric cards directly to your residence in Vizag city or Pendurthi.

Once your measurements are recorded, they are saved in your client profile for effortless reorders on ready-stitched or custom blouses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding Our Story & Process

Where are SAVIAA saree blouses stitched?

All SAVIAA blouses are hand-stitched in our master tailoring workshop located at Pendurthi Junction, Visakhapatnam, supporting experienced local Telugu tailoring artisans.

Can I bring my own saree fabric to SAVIAA for custom stitching?

Yes. You can bring your saree blouse fabric directly to our Pendurthi boutique, or schedule a doorstep measurement visit where our tailor picks up your fabric from your home in Visakhapatnam.
