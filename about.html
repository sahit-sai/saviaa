Our Story & Craft: Handcrafted Saree Blouses in Visakhapatnam

SAVIAA was founded to bring precision engineering and architectural discipline to saree blouse tailoring. Every garment cut in our Pendurthi workshop balances chest ease, armhole drop, and neck depth.

HERITAGE & CRAFT

Artisanal Telugu Tailoring

Operating out of Pendurthi Junction, our workshop employs master tailors who have dedicated decades to South Indian ethnic garment construction. Every saree blouse is measured, cut, and reinforced with cotton lining to ensure long-lasting wear.

From classic silk U-necks to intricate Maggam embroidery work, our collections marry traditional Telugu tailoring heritage with contemporary silhouette precision.

DOORSTEP SIZING

Home Measurement Appointments

Recognizing the busy schedules of women in Visakhapatnam, SAVIAA offers doorstep measurement appointments. Our female representatives visit your home in Vizag city or Pendurthi to record body metrics and pick up saree fabric.

Where are SAVIAA saree blouses stitched?

All SAVIAA blouses are hand-stitched in our master tailoring workshop located at Pendurthi Junction, Visakhapatnam, supporting experienced local Telugu tailoring artisans.
