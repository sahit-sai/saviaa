Tailoring Insights

Saree Blouse FAQ Journal

What fabric is best for custom wedding saree blouses in South India?

Pure Raw Silk and Kanchipuram Brocade Silk are the preferred fabrics for South Indian bridal blouses. They hold heavy Maggam embroidery, maintain structural shape across long wedding ceremonies, and provide breathable comfort.

How do I prevent saree blouse armholes from pulling or pinching?

Armhole pulling is avoided by taking exact arm circumference and armhole depth measurements during tailoring. SAVIAA uses curved sleeve scye cutting with a 0.5-inch ease allowance to ensure full arm movement without tugging.
